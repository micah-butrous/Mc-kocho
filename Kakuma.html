KAKUMA REFUGEE CAMP

Kakuma has been established since 1992 with the main objectives of settling displaced people of south Sudan due to serve war in south sudan in the earliest 1980s. The reception of south sudan people was initialling the lokichogo place that was the starting place of the camp in the earliest years back. Today Kakuma camp has reached with total number of about 500,000 thousand Refugees population from the following countries

  • Sudan
  • Ethiopia
  • Congo
  • Burunid
  • Tanzania
  • Eritria
  • Chad

    • United Nation High Commissioner for Refugees (UNHCR) has been the main providing agent for all people of the above countries providing them with the following:
  • Accomodation: such as shelter, water, and food.
  • Education: Refugees who felt from their country were given opportunity to join, primary, Secondary and as well University Scholarships to pursue higher Education
  • Repiatration: Some recently coutries have gotten peace at their country of origin, UNHCR has been in place giving chance to repiatrate them back to their countries
  • Resettlement: to their third Country to seek better life
