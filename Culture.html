CULTURAL PRACTICES IN KAKUMA REFUGEE CAMP

In 2014 with the increament of Refugees in the camp, many Refugees were allowed to involue in wrestling and moduling that promoted their cultures and interaction with fellow Refugees

THE MOST POPULAR CULTURAL ACITVITIES IN KAKUMA TODAY IS FOOTBALL

Kakuma Premier League is football program that is engaged by youth with aiming of promoting talents and skills amongst different Nationalities in the camp.

Fashions and moduling is the second activities that is performed by youth, involving youth male and young female.
