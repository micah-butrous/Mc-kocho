After Secondary Education, Many Refugees involve in Vocational Trainings to get skills and knowledge and the following instution have have been supporting ict trainings in kakuma for some good years downline.

Resilience Action international (RAI)

Resilience Action international (RAI) is a community Based Organization based at Kakuma 2 Zone 2 Block 2 that was formed by other Fellow Refugees in the year 2010 with the aim of empowering youth and promoting child Education with better skills.

THE FOLLOWING ARE THE PROGRMS THAT ARE CARRIED WITH RESILIENCE ACTION INTERNATIONAL.
